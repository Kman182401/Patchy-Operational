# Tasks

Your personal task list. One tap to open, one tap to check off, one tap to add.

> **How to use this file**
> - Tap a `[ ]` box to mark it done — CSS will cross it out and dim it.
> - Tap at the end of a task line, press Enter — Obsidian auto-continues the list.
> - Indent (Tab or the `→|` toolbar button) under a task to add notes about it.
> - Move finished items to `## Done` when you feel like tidying, or don't. Both work.
